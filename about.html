Curbside Rentals Inc is a leasing company near Orangeville, ON, with a fleet of curbside trucks built to increase productivity for delivery and moving businesses.

Why curbside trucks.

Our trucks are equipped with shelving, direct access to the bulkhead, and roll-up rear doors for easier loading and unloading at docks. Curbside access means your crew can safely enter and exit the vehicle without walking through traffic.

We serve Owen Sound, Barrie, and the Greater Toronto Area from our base near Orangeville.
